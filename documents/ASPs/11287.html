- Description -

Alexis Trace, otherwise known as ASP 11287 or codename 'Poltergeist' formerly known as researcher [DATA EXPUNGED], is an individual that does not exist, and will continue to not exist, until the moment someone remembers her by current name, Alexis Trace. When this occurs, she becomes existent somewhere in a short distance from the person remembering her, and if the rememberer proceeds to stop thinking about her, she will immediately cease to exist. She is capable of being remembered by multiple people at once, although this can create varying results depending mostly on whether the secondary rememberers are aware she currently exists.

When Alexis Trace is remembered back into existence, her appearance is usually a somewhat short and slightly overweight 30 year old ukranian woman in unformal clothing, although even this is inconsistent, depending mostly on how the rememberer remembers her- example, if you thought she was filipino, then when you remember her, she'll look filipino, or if you remember her in a business suit, then she'll appear in one. She will only appear dead if she is remembered as dead.

Contrary to her inconsistent appearance, she seems to keep a consistent personality and memory between all rememberances, being usually a heavy thrill seeker, and tending to make impulsive actions, although this personality greatly differs from before she gained her anomalous properties.

Additionally, Alexis Trace has the ability to telepathically communicate with anyone thinking about her, as well as the ability to teleport herself anywhere that can be seen by a rememberer. She does not seem to have an intact sense of pain, sense of exhaustion, or seem susceptible to most cognitohazards, although the cause of these is unknown.

She has proven to be very useful as a field agent, and is often assigned as a temporary field team member on missions with low chances of survival, as to avoid too many people knowing about her.
