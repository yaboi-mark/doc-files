ACE 600|wwww| - TÆXI


Class: Turbulent

- Description -

ACE 600-TOKEN is an undetermined amount of square brass coins 35 millimeters wide and 3 millimeters thick, with an engraving of a staff on one side, and a shield on the other. Aside from being excessively difficult to tarnish, a TOKEN’s only anomalous effect occurs when dropped, thrown, or otherwise placed onto any kind of road, path, or similar means of transportation.

When any instance of 600-TOKEN is dropped, thrown, or otherwise placed onto any kind of road, path, or similar means of transportation, the TOKEN will sink into the surface, and a vintage yellow 1920s-era taxicab (ACE 600-CAB) with a sign on top saying “TÆXI” will manifest on a position of the same road. CAB usually manifests 48 meters away from the token, usually traveling at an estimated 230 kilometers per hour, although the position and speed seems subject to change. CAB will rapidly slow down until it stops at the token's former position, and wait thirty seconds to let people into CAB.

Inside of 600-CAB, a young, seemingly unaging american woman (ACE 600-DRIVER) always takes the driver’s seat, usually featuring informal clothing, a vague new york accent, and a flippantly vulgar vocabulary, while simultaneously very charismatic and conversational. DRIVER will ask any boarder where they would like to travel, seemingly able to decipher any response given regardless of how vague or nondescript it is, and will accept any destination, given there is a valid road, path, or similar means of transportation near the desired location.

When a destination is provided, 600-CAB’s doors will close, and lock, and 600-DRIVER will rapidly adjust the CAB’s gear shift, and accelerate rapidly for up to 48 meters, before the CAB and its passengers spontaneously disappears as it teleports into a so far pocket dimension containing little more than a road tunnel (ACE 600-TUNNEL), so far unremarkable save for its infinite length.

After roughly ten to fifteen minutes pass, 600-CAB and its passengers will teleport to a position near the stated destination, and rapidly decelerate for up to 48 meters, before the CAB’s doors unlock, and 600-DRIVER asks the passenger(s) to leave the vehicle, before accelerating up to 48 meters and vanishing.

At any stage, a passenger can ask 600-DRIVER for any amount of ACE 600-TOKENs, and will need to pay using money, items, or more abstract concepts, such as one’s sight, a dear memory, or a soul. If a trade is agreed upon, the traded item will vanish, and the stated amount of TOKENs will appear in the passenger’s hand.

As it stands, many 600-TOKENs seem to be in civilian circulation as well as in the hands of many Groups Of Interest, although the numbers on this is unknown.
