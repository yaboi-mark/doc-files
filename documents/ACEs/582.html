ACE 582|wwww| - PenBlade



Status: Uncontained
Class: Hazardous

- Description -

At first glance, ACE 582 is a boxknife with a body of lime green plastic and an extremely sharp blade of rusted, high carbon steel, capable of cutting through thin concrete with mild effort. The object has so far shown to be indestructible, and evidence has yet to confirm if the blade's rust has had any adverse effects on its performance in both mundane and anomalous tasks, although this seems to be likely.

The primary anomalous property shows itself when the blade of ACE 582 is used to write any thaumaturgic symbol into any given object, immediately causing the symbol to glow for half a second, and then casting the spell upon the object. Beyond the initial drawing itself, this does not take any extra energy to cast, and the effect of the casted spell is largely identical to what the symbol would normally do.

ACE 582 has been reportedly seen used by various individual, and while it does seem that there is only one instance of ACE 582, it does not seem to have one consistent owner, although it's almost always seen being used to either construct anomalies or steal anomalies.

While the DOC has never actually managed to capture ACE 582, it was once in the custody of the american Department for the Concealment of the Anomalous for the duration of roughly one month, before being stolen by a currently unknown individual. We have been trying to contact the american Department for the Concealment of the Anomalous, but they take way too long to respond to our emails. Fuck the DCA.

* Marked object explodes after five seconds.
* Marked object becomes permanently incapable of being damaged until the symbol is melted off.
* Marked object melts.
* Marked object glows a bright white for one minute.
* Marked object becomes a large d20.
* Marked object is sliced by a horizontal slice of energy that travels five meters forward and cuts through objects.
* Marked object sucks in soul of the next person to die within a seven meter radius.
* Marked object becomes a hot skillet containing a freshly cooked omelet. Contents of the omelet are dependent on the object that was marked. The omelet maintains the symbol on its bottom.
* A mushroom turned into an omelet containing mushrooms and onions.
* A maple tree turned into an omelet cooked in maple syrup, with a slice of butter on top.
* A large boulder turned into a two meter long omelet filled with rough rock salt. The skillet was not upsized to hold this omelet, and it fell immediately onto the ground.

ACE 582 users have not yet attempted to steal or free any humanoid ACEs, nor have the users ever appeared in groups, although they've always worn a lime green coat.
