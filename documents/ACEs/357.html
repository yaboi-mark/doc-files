ACE 357|wwww| - Don't stare at the sun



Class: Calamity

- Description -

ACE 357 is a cognitohazard triggered by looking at the sun that causes the viewer to not only sustain damage to their eyes, but also believe that this damage is completely ordinary, and that the sun has always been this way. This phenomena began sometime around january 2000, and before that no such phenomena was documented, with the sun being slightly painful yet not damaging upon being stared at.

There is an estimated 1,500 people on earth with an immunity to this phenomenon, and of the located 437 so far, none of them have been born after january first, 2000, suggesting that these unaffected people might only decrease in number. A significant amount of resources have gone into locating the remaining immunes, and making sure that they live for as long as possible.

We do not know why this phenomena exists, but it isn't impossible that there may be something on the sun being intentionally hidden, or that there may be more, undocumented effects of this cognitohazard. Testing is ongoing.
