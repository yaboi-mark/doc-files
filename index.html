ACE 2000 is an unassuming computer of custom make, most closely resembling early IBM Personal Computer models. Its frame is made of a durable grey plastic, and the attached keyboard and mouse seem to be wholly non-anomalous in nature, and resemble keyboards and mice from roughly the same timeframe. Its screen seems to be higher resolution than expected, and primarily displays a different color, a neon shade of cyan, although it is also capable of displaying colored images, and playing high definition stereo sound.

ACE 2000 was discovered through an anonymous tip to check a seemingly unused computer in the back corner of the 3rd floor of an office building in Victoria, Canada. That segment of the office building had not been used in a while, although sources outline that the computer was likely not present at the time of the previous users of the space leaving. The computer did not contain any files upon opening.

ACE 2000 seems to possess the ability to store an unlimited amount of folders and files, and also seems to be completely immune to both anomalies (such as reality benders) and anti-anomalies (such as reality anchors), and no other device has been found to contain these properties, and attempts to recreate a device with these properties has failed.

Following incident DOC-Genesis, ACE-2000 was repurposed as Project SHADOS, and is now designed last record of everything the anomalous world has done, is doing, or would've done, ultimately being in preperation for various hypothetical scenarios, such as destruction of earth, destruction of all sentient life, destruction of the universe, destruction of all anomalies, and destruction of anything else concerned within this computer. ACE-2000's unique list of anomalous properties makes it the only object known to the DOC that can perform this task, and will hence maintain its singular burden.
